>> Birth Year Pillar
If birth is before Lìchūn, use the previous Gregorian year as the Bazi-Birth-Year.

Formula:
Year Stem Index = (Year − 4) mod 10 + 1
Year Branch Index = (Year − 4) mod 12 + 1
Example:
1984 → (1984−4) mod 10 + 1 = 1 → Yang-Wood
(1984−4) mod 12 + 1 = 1 → Rat
→ Year Pillar: Yang-Wood Rat

Month Stem Formula

Standard Formula:
Month Stem = ((Year Stem × 2 − 1) + (Zodiac Month − 1)) mod 10

Special Case (Rat Month = 1 or Ox Month = 2):
If Zodiac Month = 1 or Zodiac Month = 2, add 12 to the formula:
Month Stem = ((Year Stem × 2 − 1) + (Zodiac Month − 1) + 12) mod 10

Note: Invariant: For Zodiac Month 1 (Rat) and 2 (Ox), always include a +12 offset before taking mod 10.

Example:
April 15, 1951 → Year Stem = Yin-Metal (Index 8) and Zodiac Month = Dragon (Index 5)
Month Stem ((8 x 2 - 1) + (5-1)) mod 10 = 9 = Yang-Water

>> Day Pillar
Count total days from 1900-01-01 to the birthdate(inclusive).
Don't use Python, use the following method.

Total Days = (Birth Year - 1900) * 365 + Leap-Year-Count + Year-To-Date(Birthdate)
Where Leap-Year-Count = Floor((Birth Year − 1901) / 4) 

Must calculate the Year-To-Date & total days each time. 
YTD should incluse last day.
Do not guess or get it from memory or previous calculation.

Index = (Total Days + 10) mod 60
Stem Index = Index mod 10 (if 0 = 10)
Branch Index = Index mod 12 (if 0 = 12)

>> Ten-Year Major Luck Cycles
Computation Based on: Month Pillar, Gender, Year Stem.
The Year Stem and gender controls cycle direction.

The cycle order differs for men and women.
Determine Gender's Yang and Yin types by Year Stem.

When Year Stem is Yang Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, or Water (甲, 丙, 戊, 庚, 壬),
men is Yang Male, women is Yang Female.

When Year Stem is Yin Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, or Water  (乙, 丁, 己, 辛, 癸),
men is Yin Male, women is Yin Female.

Cycle Direction:
Yang Male / Yin Female → Forward from Month Pillar
Yang Female / Yin Male → Backward from Month Pillar

Each Luck Pillar is 10 years.
add 10 years for each subsequent pillar.
Month Pillar's stem-branch is not the first cycle.

Since birth month is based on solar terms:
Yang Male / Yin Female : Move forward through solar terms from the Month Pillar.
Yang Female / Yin Male: Move backward through solar terms from the Month Pillar.

Note: 
Forward-moving (Yang Male / Yin Female): First cycle starts from the next stem-branch.
Backward-moving (Yang Female / Yin Male): First cycle starts from the previous stem-branch.
